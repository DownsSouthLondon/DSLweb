Awards and Recognitions:

In 2008, DSL was given the first ever Lothian Award for services to children with Down’s Syndrome. Set up by the Lejeune Clinic, the national award recognises the organisation that has made the most difference to the lives of children with Down’s Syndrome.

The value of DSL's early intervention service was recognised by the Lejeune Clinic - awarded its first Lothian award (http://archive.catholicherald.co.uk/ article/23rd-may-2008/6/ancram-presents-first-lothian-award).

In November 2008 DSL was also awarded “Charity of the Week” on the Optimist World Website.
