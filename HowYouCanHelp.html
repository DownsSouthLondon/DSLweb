How You Can Help...



What it costs

DSL currently requires around £78,000 per year to provide its existing therapy service:
  • £70,000 for the therapy service
  • £6,000 for hiring the facility in which the services are provided
  • £2,000 for specialist resource materials to support therapy

Our future plans

In addition to raising the funds required each year to fund its ongoing services, DSL’s ambition is to double its annual donations in order to:

  • Extend its service to a greater number of children and reduce its waiting list
  • Expand the age range of children that it covers; and
  • Implement bridging programmes and specialist outreach advice into schools attended by older children.
